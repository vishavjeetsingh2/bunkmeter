2. Accuracy of Calculations

BunkMeter's calculations are mathematically exact given the inputs provided. However, the accuracy of the output depends entirely on the accuracy of the data you enter. Common sources of discrepancy include:

· Your college's portal may not have updated its records in real time.
· Your institution may use a different rounding or aggregation method.
· Some colleges track attendance by session (morning/afternoon) rather than by lecture.
· Medical leaves, duty leaves, or exempt sessions may be handled differently by different institutions.
· Attendance cut-off dates may cause your college's count to differ from your own running tally.

We strongly recommend using BunkMeter as a planning aid, not as a substitute for checking your official portal.
