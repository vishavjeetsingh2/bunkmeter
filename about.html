About

Built for the 8am lecture you're deciding on right now.

BunkMeter exists because every Indian college student has, at some point, done frantic mental math to figure out if they can skip a class without falling below the attendance cutoff. We just did the math for you.

Why we built this

Attendance rules are strict, colleges rarely make the numbers easy to check in real time, and the consequences of guessing wrong — detention, exam ineligibility — are serious. Most students end up doing rough percentage math on a calculator app between classes, hoping they got it right.

BunkMeter turns that guesswork into an exact answer: enter your attended and total classes, your college's minimum percentage, and how many classes are left in the term. You instantly get either a safe bunk budget or a recovery plan telling you exactly how many classes you need to attend to get back above the line.

What makes it different

  • No sign-up, no login — open it and use it immediately.
  • Runs entirely in your browser. Your numbers never leave your device.
  • Includes a condonation calculator for medical certificates, NCC/NSS, and sports duty days.
  • Works on any device, any browser, and can be added to your phone's home screen.
